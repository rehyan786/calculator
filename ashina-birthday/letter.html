My dearest

Ashina

Happy birthday, my love. Today the whole world gets to celebrate the person I've quietly been grateful for every single day — not because it's your birthday, but because of what it reminds me: that the day you were born is the reason my life looks this warm, this soft, this much more mine.

I love the way you laugh at your own jokes before you even finish telling them. I love how you remember the tiny things I mention once and forget I ever said. I love that you make ordinary Tuesdays feel like something worth showing up for, and that your kindness doesn't switch off around people who "don't matter."

So here's my wish for you this year: may it be softer on the hard days, louder on the joyful ones, and full of every small, ridiculous happiness you deserve. Thank you for choosing me, again and again, on your way to becoming exactly who you are. I'll be right here, cheering the loudest.

With all my heart,

Shadman
